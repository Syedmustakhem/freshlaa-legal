Refund & Cancellation Policy – Freshlaa

Last updated: 2026

This Refund & Cancellation Policy explains how Freshlaa handles order cancellations, refunds, and non-refundable items.

Order Cancellation

  • Orders can be cancelled before dispatch.
  • Once an order has been dispatched, cancellation may not be possible.

Refunds

  • If payment is successful but the order fails, a refund will be initiated.
  • Refunds are processed to the original payment method used at checkout.
  • Refund timelines depend on the payment provider and typically take 5–7 working days.

Non-Refundable Items

  • Perishable goods.
  • Items that have already been delivered.
