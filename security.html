Freshlaa is committed to protecting your data and maintaining a secure platform. This Security Policy explains the measures we take to safeguard your information and transactions.

Your security is our priority. We follow industry best practices to protect user data and prevent unauthorized access.

Data Protection

  • All sensitive data is transmitted over secure HTTPS connections.
  • User information is stored in protected servers with restricted access.
  • Passwords and authentication data are encrypted.

Payment Security

  • Freshlaa does not store card or UPI details.
  • Payments are handled by trusted third-party payment gateways.
  • Payment providers comply with PCI-DSS security standards.

Account Security

  • OTP-based login and verification mechanisms are used.
  • Users are responsible for keeping login credentials confidential.
  • Suspicious activity may result in temporary account suspension.

Fraud Prevention

  • Automated systems monitor suspicious activity.
  • Accounts involved in misuse or fraud may be restricted or terminated.

Data Access Control

Access to user data is limited to authorized personnel only and used strictly for service-related purposes.

Third-Party Services

Freshlaa may use third-party services for analytics, hosting, and payments. These services are selected based on their security standards and compliance.

Security Updates

We regularly review and improve our security practices to protect against evolving threats.

Reporting Security Issues

If you discover a security vulnerability, please report it immediately.

  • Email: security@freshlaa.in
  • Support: support@freshlaa.in
